Social media refers to a collection of online platforms and tools that allow users to create, share, and exchange information, ideas, and content in virtual communities and networks. Examples of social media platforms include Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and YouTube.

Social media has become an integral part of modern life, with billions of people around the world using these platforms to connect with friends, family, and others, share news and information, and discover new ideas and interests. Social media has also transformed the way businesses and organizations interact with their customers and audiences, providing new opportunities for marketing, customer service, and brand building.

However, social media also has its downsides, such as the spread of misinformation and the potential for cyberbullying and harassment. It is important for users to be mindful of the content they share and consume on social media and to use these platforms responsibly.
